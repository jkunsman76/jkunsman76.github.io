Final Capstone Project

Music City Classics

Music City Classics is a Mobile Friendly Web application developed to help bring the classic car community in Nashville closer together and share knowledge. Through this application users can share what they are working on with other users and ask for help when stuck trying to figure something out. Users can also signup to join other users events or host their own event! Sharing knowledge and coming together as a community is what will keep the classic car industry alive for the next generation of enthusiasts. Did I mention its mobile friendly?

Capstone Project

Intents

InTents is a camping application designed to be easy to use and minimalistic in nature, just like camping. It allows users to interact in real time with campsites and see up-to-date information about the places they plan to visit on their next adventure. Never before has planning what to bring and how to be prepared been so simple.
